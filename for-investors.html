For Investors & Buyers

Assess policy risk and allocate capital with confidence

Carbon credit investments are subject to significant policy risk. Regulatory changes can affect credit supply, pricing, and project viability. Countries that lack clear Article 6 frameworks present higher authorization risk and longer timelines.

Stratus provides the analysis investors need to:

  • Evaluate country risk across jurisdictions
  • Model portfolio exposure to policy and regulatory changes
  • Assess credit supply under different policy scenarios
  • Conduct due diligence on project pipelines
  • Understand demand drivers across voluntary and compliance markets

Country Risk Assessment

We evaluate:

  • Article 6 framework status and authorization processes
  • Regulatory stability and policy predictability
  • Institutional capacity and approval timelines
  • Revenue sharing requirements and foreign investment rules
  • Track record of government engagement with carbon projects

This analysis allows investors to allocate capital to jurisdictions with lower regulatory risk and clearer pathways to credit issuance.

Portfolio Modeling

We model:

  • Credit supply from project pipelines by geography and type
  • Price sensitivity to policy changes and market demand
  • Correlation of policy risk across jurisdictions
  • Break-even pricing under different regulatory and cost scenarios

Models support portfolio construction, hedging strategies, and scenario planning.

Market Intelligence

Subscribers receive:

  • Quarterly updates on Article 6 developments and government decisions
  • Analysis of credit supply and demand dynamics
  • Tracking of authorization activity and policy changes
  • Forward-looking assessments of regulatory risk

Due Diligence Support

We conduct country-level due diligence for specific investments, including:

  • Regulatory pathway analysis
  • Stakeholder mapping and government engagement assessment
  • Policy risk evaluation
  • Review of authorization status and timeline

Access

Market intelligence and country risk assessments are available by subscription. Due diligence and custom modeling are delivered through advisory engagements.
