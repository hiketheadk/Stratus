About Stratus

Building confidence in carbon markets through policy analysis

Stratus is a specialized advisory and intelligence platform focused on the intersection of carbon market policy, climate finance, and project development. We serve governments, investors, and developers who need to understand how policy shapes market outcomes.

Carbon markets are expanding rapidly, but policy fragmentation and regulatory uncertainty remain barriers to deployment of capital. Countries are adopting different approaches to Article 6, voluntary markets operate under varied standards, and project economics depend heavily on local regulatory context.

Stratus was created to address this gap. We combine climate policy expertise, financial modeling, and government affairs experience to help clients:

  • Assess country readiness for Article 6 and voluntary carbon markets
  • Model how policy changes affect project economics and carbon credit supply
  • Navigate authorization processes and reduce regulatory risk
  • Design national frameworks that attract investment while maintaining integrity

We work with climate ministries, development banks, institutional investors, carbon project developers, and policy research institutions. Our approach is grounded in market fundamentals, regulatory analysis, and long-term capital allocation.

Our Approach

We do not advocate for specific climate policies. We analyze how existing and proposed policies affect market structure, capital flows, and project viability. Our work is designed to support informed decision-making by governments, investors, and developers.

Parent Organization

Stratus is a specialized platform operated by Carbon Capital Advisors, an advisory firm focused on carbon market development and climate finance.
