Modeling & Market Intelligence

Financial and policy modeling for carbon market decisions

Stratus provides modeling tools and market intelligence that help investors and developers evaluate carbon projects under different policy and price scenarios.

Financial Modeling

We model project economics based on:

  • Carbon credit volumes and issuance schedules
  • Price assumptions across voluntary and compliance markets
  • Cost structures (development, monitoring, verification, government fees)
  • Revenue sharing and tax treatment
  • Policy risk and regulatory timelines

Models are customizable by project type (nature-based solutions, engineered removals, avoided emissions), geography, and financing structure.

Policy Impact Analysis

We assess how changes in policy affect:

  • Credit supply from specific jurisdictions
  • Project viability and break-even pricing
  • Demand from compliance buyers and voluntary markets
  • Authorization timelines and transaction costs

This analysis supports portfolio construction, risk assessment, and market forecasting.

Market Intelligence

Subscribers receive:

  • Quarterly updates on Article 6 implementation and policy developments
  • Supply and demand analysis by credit type and geography
  • Tracking of government authorization decisions and pipeline activity
  • Regulatory risk assessments for key jurisdictions

Access

Modeling tools and intelligence reports are available through subscription. Custom analysis and scenario planning are available through advisory engagements.
