Policy clarity for carbon markets

Stratus provides governments, investors, and project developers with the analysis they need to navigate Article 6, strengthen national carbon frameworks, and deploy climate finance at scale.

Carbon markets operate at the intersection of policy, finance, and climate science. Stratus translates regulatory complexity into actionable intelligence—helping countries build credible frameworks, investors assess risk, and developers structure viable projects.
