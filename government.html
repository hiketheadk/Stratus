For Governments

Strategic support for governments building credible carbon market frameworks

Stratus advises governments on the design and implementation of national carbon market policies. We help countries structure frameworks that attract investment, maintain environmental integrity, and align with international commitments.

Our Services

Article 6 Framework Design

  • Authorization processes and institutional arrangements
  • Baseline setting and corresponding adjustment methodologies
  • Revenue sharing and benefit distribution mechanisms
  • Registry and tracking systems

Stakeholder Engagement

  • Consultations with project developers and investors
  • Coordination with multilateral institutions (UNFCCC, World Bank, regional development banks)
  • Engagement with civil society and local communities

Capacity Building

  • Training for designated national authorities
  • Development of MRV protocols and verification standards
  • Support for accreditation and technical assessments

Market Readiness

  • Gap analysis and policy benchmarking
  • Investment climate assessments
  • Development of transparent authorization processes
  • Support for international negotiations and bilateral agreements

How We Work

Engagements typically begin with a readiness assessment. We identify gaps in policy, capacity, or infrastructure, then develop a phased implementation plan. We work closely with climate ministries, environment agencies, and finance officials, often in coordination with multilateral partners.

Our approach prioritizes:

  • Regulatory clarity and predictability
  • Alignment with international standards
  • Transparency in decision-making
  • Long-term investor confidence
