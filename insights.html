Research & Insights

Analysis of carbon market policy and Article 6 implementation

Stratus publishes research on carbon market policy, Article 6 developments, and the intersection of climate finance and government regulation.

Research Focus

Our analysis covers:

  • Article 6 implementation across jurisdictions
  • National carbon market frameworks and policy design
  • Regulatory trends affecting credit supply and demand
  • Investment conditions and country readiness assessments
  • Multilateral engagement and bilateral cooperation

Publications

We publish:

  • Country Profiles: In-depth assessments of national frameworks
  • Policy Briefs: Analysis of regulatory developments and their market implications
  • Market Updates: Quarterly reports on credit supply, demand, and policy trends
  • Thematic Reports: Cross-country analysis of specific policy issues
